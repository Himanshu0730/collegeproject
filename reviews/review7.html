BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India)

BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India): BGMI is a popular battle royale game specifically designed for Indian players. It offers intense multiplayer matches on a large map where players fight to be the last one standing.
With its smooth gameplay, realistic graphics, and regular updates, BGMI has quickly gained a dedicated fanbase. It provides thrilling combat and strategic gameplay, making it a top choice for battle royale enthusiasts.


After much controversy, it seems like Krafton has finally managed to bring back PUBG Mobile to India but in a different form which it is calling Battlegrounds Mobile India. While Krafton claims that Battlegrounds Mobile India has been made from the ground up for the Indian audience, the posters and teasers up until now seem to show the same assets as PUBG Mobile.
And while the developers have not yet confirmed when Battlegrounds Mobile India will officially launch in India, the beta version of the game is live for those that are in the early access program. Hence we can expect that the game will be made live for everyone sooner than later.
